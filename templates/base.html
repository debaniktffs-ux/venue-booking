Guidelines & Operational Workflow

Internal Records

This portal serves as the primary institutional ledger for venue allocation. Every reservation recorded here constitutes an official intent for venue usage and is used for audit and scheduling purposes.

Approval Workflow

Securing a slot on the dashboard is the first step. Once a reservation is made:

  • A Mail Template is automatically triggered in the sidebar.
  • Users must send this template to the requested faculty member.
  • Official approval is only granted once the faculty responds affirmatively to this email.

Record Management

Integrity of data is crucial. If a request is denied or cancelled, users are strictly required to delete the corresponding entry from the 'Booking History' immediately. Approved records must be maintained as active.

Submission Protocol

Reservations must be submitted by authorized club representatives only. Unauthorized submissions will be flagged and removed.
